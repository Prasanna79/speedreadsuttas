# Launch Notes and Source Notes

Date: 2026-03-07

## Practical Notes

1. Use a personal Reddit account, not a product-branded throwaway.
2. Do not cross-post all versions at once in a spammy pattern.
3. Stay in the thread and respond like a human.
4. Lead with story and problem, not “check out my app”.

## Source Notes Used

1. `r/sideprojects` published updated rules in June 2025 saying project advertising is allowed but blatant spam is not.
2. `r/webdevelopment` monthly feedback thread states personal project feedback belongs in the monthly thread, not as self-promo spam.
3. Recent `r/Meditation` removals explicitly state self-promotion is not allowed for apps, websites, blogs, services, or products.
4. Recent `r/theravada` removals explicitly mention self-promotional content being disallowed.
5. Recent `r/InternetIsBeautiful` removals reference strong anti-self-promotion enforcement and a 90/10 participation expectation.
