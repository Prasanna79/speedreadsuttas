# SuttaSpeed Launch Marketing Spec v1

Date: 2026-03-07
Product URL: `https://suttaspeed.com`
Direct demo URL: `https://suttaspeed.com/read/mn19/en/sujato`

## 1. Goal

Drive qualified traffic to `suttaspeed.com` by launching with:

1. one short-form X post
2. several Reddit posts tailored to different communities
3. a personal founder story rather than a generic product launch tone

## 2. Core Narrative

The message should center on:

1. personal frustration with how hard it is to read long suttas on screens
2. the idea that RSVP and chunked reading can reduce friction
3. a concrete live demo people can click immediately

## 3. Audience Segments

### 3.1 Buddhist / sutta readers

What they care about:

1. access to the texts
2. practical study help
3. translation choice
4. low-friction reading

### 3.2 Builders / indie makers

What they care about:

1. personal build story
2. niche product clarity
3. implementation and UX decisions
4. launch authenticity

### 3.3 Web/product people

What they care about:

1. unusual interface design
2. typography, pacing, focus mode
3. speed-reading or interaction design angle

## 4. Positioning

Recommended positioning:

1. not “AI Buddhism”
2. not “meditation app”
3. not “general speed-reading app”
4. instead: “a focused RSVP reader for suttas”

## 5. Message Components

Each post should include some version of:

1. founder story
2. clear problem
3. what the product does
4. practical benefits
5. direct link to one live sutta
6. invitation for feedback or usage

## 6. Founder Story Requirements

The founder story should sound personal and specific:

1. I wanted to actually read more suttas, not just save tabs
2. Long passages on a screen felt harder to stay with than they should
3. I wanted a way to keep my eyes and attention on the text instead of constantly scanning
4. So I built a reader designed around one thing: actually getting through a sutta

## 7. Benefit Framing

Benefits to mention:

1. less visual scanning
2. easier to keep attention
3. adjustable speed and chunking
4. translation switching
5. mobile-friendly and fast to try

## 8. Link Strategy

Default CTA link:

1. `https://suttaspeed.com/read/mn19/en/sujato`

Reason:

1. it gets users directly into the product
2. it reduces homepage bounce for launch traffic

## 9. Reddit Strategy v1

Potential targets:

1. `r/SideProject`
2. `r/sideprojects`
3. `r/webdevelopment`
4. `r/alphaandbetausers`

Potentially avoid:

1. `r/Meditation`
2. `r/theravada`
3. `r/Buddhism`

Reason:

1. these communities often remove self-promotional links or site launches

## 10. Tone

1. personal
2. calm
3. concrete
4. not hypey
5. not growth-hacker-ish

## 11. Success Metric

Primary:

1. qualified clicks to the direct MN19 reader

Secondary:

1. comments from people who actually try it
2. product feedback
3. repeat visits
